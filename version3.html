Platform & Priorities

As Board Chair, Steve will prioritize
what matters to Putnam County families.

I

End the "Bait & Switch"

Rezonings must be Project-Specific. What's promised is what must be built—or the process starts over. No more switching plans after approval is granted. Your neighborhood deserves the development that was actually proposed.

II

Stop Commercial Invasion

Residential neighborhoods are for families, not commercial sprawl. Steve will draw and defend clear lines between community spaces and commercial development pressure— protecting the character of Putnam County for generations to come.

Your tax dollars deserve disciplined, executive-level budgeting. Steve brings proven financial stewardship honed over six years as Commission Chairman—no waste, no shortcuts, no excuses.
