While others may offer promises, Steve offers proven results:

As Board Chair, Steve will prioritize what matters most:

  • FISCAL ACCOUNTABILITY With 6 years as your former Commission Chair, Steve treats your tax dollars with executive-level discipline. He knows how to manage a budget so taxpayers don't get stuck with the bill for growth.

Putnam County doesn't have time for a learning curve. We need a Chairman who has already led the Board of Education, chaired the County Commission, and spent 30 years protecting our community.
